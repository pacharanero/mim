1    Coding and Indexing

This document lists the CRE types and which templates they may contain for each CDA domain.

 

 

For Medications and Allergies, coded entries are required but not mandatory and likewise for indexing against the relevant CRE types for meds and allergies.

For all items other than Medications and Allergies, coding is optional, but CRE types must not be used, except and until CFH provides explicit guidance on their use. These CRE types are greyed out below

 

The Implementation Guidance for Suppliers contains further information  regarding CRE types.
