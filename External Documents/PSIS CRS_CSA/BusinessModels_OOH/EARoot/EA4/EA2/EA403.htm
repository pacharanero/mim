This is the senior clinician for the clinic/centre. They will be the responsible party for any documents sent by non-clinical users.

This is likely to be a fixed/system level maintained item and will may be someone like the Medical Director for the centre. It is not expected that the user will be required to be logged in to the system when a document is created or sent.
