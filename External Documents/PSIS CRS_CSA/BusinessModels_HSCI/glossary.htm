Item Type Meaning
ACS Business Access Control Service This service holds the "Consent to Share" Flag.
Association Technical A relationship between two or more entities. Implies a connection of some type - for example one entity uses the services of another, or one entity is connected to another over a network link.
BIM Technical Business Information Model Is a basic overview of the information concepts and their relationships with the business area or domain, this is represented diagrammatically.
BPM Technical Shows one, many or all possible configurations of the Detailed Use Cases within the domain.
Business Application Role Technical Defines the points of origin/destination of business interactions to meet a defined business need. Application roles must be defined for sending and receiving, and are therefore paired. The Business Application Roles may be implemented by many systems which are referred to as ’Host Systems‘ within the Interaction Unit.
CAF Business Common Assessment Framework - Assessment and Care planning for all adult groups
Care/ Support Plan Business A document detailing the agreed actions/ services/ treatment/ care/ support that will be provided to support the needs identified during assessment.
Carer Business A carer is a person (usually friend or family member) that provides regular and substantive care to an individual. N.B. this term is NOT applied to paid/voluntary support workers.
CD Technical Communication Description A diagram showing the classes, attributes, cardinalities and associations (held within the CIM package) required for a particular communication.
CIM Technical Communication Information Model A package containing classes and associations required to structure and populate a Communication Description for each communication and where there is a related group of communications to illustrate those relationships. The Communication Descriptions populated with these classes and associations are held within this package.
Class Technical A logical entity encapsulating data and behaviour. A class is a template for an object - the class is the design, the object the runtime instance.
CPA Business Care Programme Approach
CRE Business Care Record Element
CSSR Business Council with Social Services Responsibilities: Local Authority with statutory responsibility to provide social care services.
DCSF/ DfES Business Department for Education and Skills: Health & Social Care are the care and support services that provide care for those who have short or long-term ongoing needs. For the purpose of this project this is limited at this time to the Statutory services delivered by the NHS and Councils with Social Services Responsibilities (CSSRs)
Detailed Use Case Model Technical Consists of a use case diagram and Detailed Use Cases. A Detailed Use Case Model will derive from a use case within the Overview Use Case Model. There may be multiple Detailed Use Case Models; where there are, the parent must be one of the use cases identified in the overview model
ESCR Business Electronic Social Care Record - Policy Initiative from Department of Health which should enable the electronic capture and storage of structured, unstructured and coded data from social care records within Local Authorities
Extends Relationship Technical A relationship between two use cases in which one use case 'extends' the behaviour of another. Typically this represents optional behaviour in a use case scenario - for example a user may optionally request a list or report at some point in a performing a business use case.
FACS Business Fair Access to Care Services - Eligibility criteria, used by Social Services Departments to determine whether a person is eligible for services provided by them. The framework is based on an individual’s needs and associated risks to independence, and includes four eligibility bands - critical, substantial, moderate and low.
HSC or H&SC Business Health & Social Care: Health & Social Care are the care and support services that provide care for those who have short or long-term ongoing needs. For the purpose of this project this is limited at this time to the Statutory services delivered by the NHS and CSSRs.
HSCI Business Health and Social Care Integration
IAM Technical Information Analysis Model A data model showing the classes, associations, cardinalities and attributes for a particular area of interest.
ICS Business Integrated Children's System
IE Technical Information Example Is a complete and valid collection of communication content that supports a Scenario and is structured in accordance with a corresponding Communication Description. Information Examples are expressed as Object models such that each object model represents an instance of its corresponding Communication Description.
Includes Relationship Technical A relationship between two use cases in which one use case 'includes' the behaviour. This is indicated where there a specific business use cases which are used from many other places - for example updating a train record may be part of many larger business processes.
Interaction Unit Technical A Sequence Diagram describing a set of related business interactions between one pair of Business Application Roles.
LR Business Legitimate Relationship
NHS CRS Business National Health Service Care Records Service
Overview Assessment Business An assessment which considers the person, their situation and needs in the entirety and is not limited in focus by service type or specialty.
Overview Business Process Model Technical An activity diagram showing the high-level domain processes/flows, and actors (as swim lanes).
Overview Use Case Model Technical Provides the start point for functional analysis which will be elaborated into a Detailed Use Case Model. This product is composed of a use case diagram and accompanying short summary descriptions.
PDS Business Personal Demographics Service
PSIS Technical Personal Spine Information Service
RBAC Business Role Based Access Control
SAP Business Single Assessment Process
Scenario Technical Scenarios are example instances of domain (business) processes/activities in narrative format.
SCR Business Summary Care Record. National record held on PSIS
SDS Business Spine Directory of Services
SSB Business Spine Security Broker The SSB Provides services for use by Spine sub-systems that support authentication and authorisation. It is used by SDS for those services. It uses SDS to obtain user information.
TMS Business Transaction Messaging Service
Use Case Technical A Use Case represents a discrete unit of interaction between a user (human or machine) and the system. A Use Case is a single unit of meaningful work; for example creating a train, modifying a train and creating orders are all Use Cases. Each Use Case has a description which describes the functionality that will be built in the proposed system. A Use Case may 'include' another Use Case's functionality or 'extend' another Use Case with its own behaviour. Use Cases are typically related to 'actors'. An actor is a human or machine entity that interacts with the system to perform meaningful work.
Use Case Activity Diagram Technical An activity diagram is used to describe a process in terms of the sequence of its activities and flow of control between the activities. Decisions, actors and other information can also be represented in the diagrams. The Use Case Activity Diagram is bounded by its Detailed Use Case.
Use Case Palette Technical A representation of the set of Detailed Use Case Models and actors for the overall model Each package contained within the palette is equivalent to a Detailed Use Case Model. The Detailed Use Cases within each package will be derived from the candidate use cases.
