The carePlan template is a complex template containing coded information for the entire care plan, however the structure of the text should be divided using multiple soft-headings.

Therefore, the template is recorded against the mainDetails class even though the actual text is recorded in the sub text-sections. Please note the sub text-sections may have additional templates associated with them.

Constraint Type Status Detail
Use of structured or unstructured approach Constraint Approved
It is expected that a care plan will follow one of 3 routes
1) use of summary cda_TextSection to carry text only care plan
2) use of componentDetails cda_TextSection to carry structured care plan
3) use of both of these sections to provide a summary and structured detail.
