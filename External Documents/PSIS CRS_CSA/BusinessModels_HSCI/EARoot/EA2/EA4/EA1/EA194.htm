This includes process of discharge from services (both non-residential and residential services).

Also includes discharge notifications as per Community Care (Delayed Discharges) Act 2003:
- Section 2: notification from Hospital to social care that a person requires an assessment, as support may be required on discharge from hospital.
- Section 5: notification from hospital to social care that a person who requires support on discharge from hospital, will soon be discharged.

Also covers death processes including notifications.
