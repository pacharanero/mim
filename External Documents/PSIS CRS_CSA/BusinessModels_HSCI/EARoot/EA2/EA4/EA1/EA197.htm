Electronic Social Care Record.
This is a requirement for CSSRs to meet, but at varying levels of implementation across the country.

Should enable the electronic capture and storage of structured, unstructured and coded data, including:

- core data
- referral, inc. material related to each referral
- assessment, inc. material related to each assessment
- care plan, inc. material related to each care plan
- review, inc. material related to each review
- registers
- complaints/complements
- service info, including provider’s information, visits and contacts
- outgoing correspondence, inc.. email
- incoming correspondence, inc.. email
- financial info
- risk/health & safety information
- archive material
