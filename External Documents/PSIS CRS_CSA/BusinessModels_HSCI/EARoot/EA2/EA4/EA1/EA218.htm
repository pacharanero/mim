The review process involves looking at the original assessment and care plan, to determine how well the current services/ care plan/ action plan are meeting the needs identified during assessment. Reviews can be either scheduled or unscheduled.

The review process is on-going, but may involve an in-depth re-assessment of needs where circumstances have changed and needs are not being met and/or services are no longer appropriate etc.

Review may also include observation and on-going monitoring of an individual.
