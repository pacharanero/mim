This involves maintaining and sharing information relating to delivering care such as:

1) Shared list of names and contact details of practitioners involved with the person (This information should be available to all interested Health and Social Care practitioners)

2) Create/ Access/Update/ Remove Admissions notifications (The admission notification that is routinely passed from Hospital to GP is also be passed to Social Care)

3) Create/ Access/Update/ Remove A&E attendance notifications (The attendance notification that is routinely passed from Hospital to GP is also be passed to Social Care)

4) Create/ Access/Update/ Remove discharge reports (The discharge report that is routinely passed from Hospital to GP may also accessed by Social Care)

5) Create/ Access/Update/ Remove Section 2/ Section 5 of Community Care Act Discharge Notifications (Generated by Hospitals, this must be passed to Social Care)

6) Create/ Access/Update/ Remove and Section 117 of Mental Health Act notifications (Generated by Mental Health Acute services, this must be passed to Social Care)

7) Access to Social Care Care plan, including service time-table (Care plan created by Social Care made available to Health Care Practitioners)
