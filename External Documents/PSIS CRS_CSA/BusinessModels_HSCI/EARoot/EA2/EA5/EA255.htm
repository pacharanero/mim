This involves maintaining and sharing information such as:

1) Access to medical history, current diagnosis, list of medications (Information as recorded by GP available to Social Care)

2) Access to Continuing Care Assessment (Assessment Documentation created by Health Care practitioner also provided to Social care)

3) Access referral outcome summaries.
(both posting to Summary Care Record and to originating referrer.)
