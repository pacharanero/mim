At the point of review it may be decided that the existing care package is meeting/ working towards meeting all needs and therefore the care package will continue un-altered.

If the care package is no longer appropriate (Needs may have changed/ may no longer be eligible/ person may wish to discontinue services etc) further decisions must be made.
