When the system has identified that a new recipient of a document is required then a copy is only sent to the new recipient.


Issues with HSCI061 (Revised):
1) users do not want to receive a new version of a doc, just because it has been sent to someone new,
2) there is no way for users to know what has changed - and would waste time trying to figure it out.
3) users possibly overloaded with new versions of docs, and don't read them at all (clinical safety risk
4) users will not know who view documents on PSIS anyway
