The Social care management system must meet the Information Governance requirements of the Existing Systems Programme.

note: The system needs to conform to the IG v3 which consist of IGv2 plus Sealing Requirements and Dissent to Store support. IGv3 foundation module applies to any system accessing PSIS/SCR from 2008A onwards.
