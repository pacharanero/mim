NHS CFH position on CRE Types at the time this document has been produced

"Medications and Allergies and Adverse Reactions must be coded using SNOMEDCT and must be indexed against the respective CRE Types. Information other than Medication or Allergies and Adverse Reactions can be text only or text and equivalent codes, but must not be indexed against CRE types, until such time as NHS CFH provides formal guidance on the use of additional CRE Types"
