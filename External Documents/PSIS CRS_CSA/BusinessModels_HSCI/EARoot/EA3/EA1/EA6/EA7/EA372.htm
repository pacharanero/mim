In some cases people may be present during an assessment or care planning event who do not take part in the event.

These people are represented on the business analysis artefacts as "personPresent" - (in HL7 this is the WITNESS participation)

The system should use this participation to carry coded information about other people present, and should include the details of these people in the Administrative Details Text section of the document.

This applies to:
- Contact Assessment
- Overview Assessment
- Specialist Assessment Outcome
- Integrated Care and Support Plan
