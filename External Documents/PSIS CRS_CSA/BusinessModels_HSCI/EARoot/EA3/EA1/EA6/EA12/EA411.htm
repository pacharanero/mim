The system may record copy recipients (in HL7 CDA this is represented as the Tracker participation) on the Integrated Care and Support Plan Document (these must be SDS entities) and also on the care plan template which allows other roles of recipient (i.e. non-SDS entities).

The two may be used in conjunction where:

- recipients who are not SDS entities have been given paper copies of the plan and SDS recipients have received electronic copies of the document.

- multiple care plan templates (e.g. for a care plan and a crisis plan) are used within a single Integrated Care and Support Plan document, if these different plans have been given to different recipients.
