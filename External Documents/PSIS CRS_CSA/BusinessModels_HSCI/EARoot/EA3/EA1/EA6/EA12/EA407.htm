In some cases the person or organisation responsible for a component of the care plan may also be the provider.

In this case the system should apply a rule on how the two are populated and rendered. For example:
If responsible person is also the provider:
- repeat information, or
- leave blank.

Suppliers should consider how this would be interpreted and consult with user groups as to how practitioners want to see this.

Worked Example:
MiM 7 HSCI Domain, section 7.4.2 Scenario 2 - Integrated Care and Support Plan example.
The split between responsibility and provider is often shown as the person (responsible) and their organisation (provider). This is correct, but person/ their org is not the only way this can be split. Some of the actions have a named social worker as both responsible and provider - this may be difficult to interpret and may seem inconsistent, but is correct.
