Summary of Assessment [assessmentSummary]

Assessor’s Overview/Formulation

Whilst managing relatively well at home, does have difficulty with personal care and has also identified social isolation as an issue for her. Reports that her son would be happy for her to live with him, however, she would like to remain independent and living in her home for as long as possible. Is happy to accept a care package to assist with this aim.

Overall Impact

Meets FACS criteria at Substantial risk to independence due to poor mobility, falls risk and inability to manage personal care independently.

Summary of Strengths and Protective Factors
Is willing to accept a care package to assist her to remain living at home independently. In addition, she has a supportive family who are willing to assist her as required.

Summary of Key Problems/Need
Requires assistance with personal care.
