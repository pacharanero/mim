Care Plan Component                                                        [medicationManagement : carePlanComponent]
Issue: Medication Management.
Tony needs to take medication regularly to treat his mental illness.

Aims and Objectives:
(1) Tony to take medication regularly and safely,
(2)  Tony to be prescribed appropriate amounts of medication that successfully treat his illness and keep side effects to a minimum.

Interventions & Actions:
(1)  Education regarding need to take medication regularly. Information about medication used and how to take and store it safely.
(2) Regular monitoring of efficacy and side effects,
(3)  Regular blood tests for Clozapine prescriptions - blood results sent to Clozapine monitoring service.
(4) Blood tests for lithium levels
(5) Prescriptions for clozapine to be issued by CMHT medical staff following clearance from clozapine monitoring service.
(6) All other prescriptions to be issued by GP.
(7) Liaison with community pharmacist to ensure their understanding of clozapine prescription process and regular supply of medication 
(8) Regular monitoring of weight, blood pressure and blood sugar levels,
(9) Education regarding need to maintain a healthy lifestyle, including exercise regime. GP to be advised of responsibility.

Frequency of Contact:
(1) Weekly visits from CMHN
(2) 6 weekly outpatient appointments with Consultant Psychiatrist 
(3) Attendance at Clozapine clinic for blood tests - variable frequency depending on results
(4) Fortnightly attendances at GP surgery to see practice nurse
(5) Weekly prescriptions from GP to be collected by Tony from local pharmacy.
