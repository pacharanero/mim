NOTE ON 999 CALLS

In all of these scenarios 999 calls are dealt with as follows.

Call is kept open. NHS-D person calls 999 and this becomes a three way conference call: NHS-D, patient and ambulance. Ambulance gets key location information from NHS-D and then ambulance confirms with patient at which point NHS-D drops out.

This can happen at any point in a nurse call or a CH call but in the case of streaming for CH it is much more likely to occur at 999 streamed.
