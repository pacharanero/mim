999 calls- The will be no messaging for 999 calls, as such calls are transferred directly to the 999 operator.

Directly transferred GP OOH calls - Such calls will be directly transferred to the approbate OOH service, who will be responsible for messaging to PSIS if appropriate. NHS Direct are employed by some OOH services to handle their calls.

If a caller is, however, referred to an OOH provider, but not directly transferred, then NHS Direct will potentially message to PSIS.
