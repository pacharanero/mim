An indirectly bookable service is one that that is not integrated with Choose and Book. Thus professional users and patients are not able to view available appointments and book via Choose and Book. The patient must telephone the Booking Manager at the service. The booking manager searches for a slot on the PAS and records this manually in Choose and Book.
